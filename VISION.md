# Vision

## Mission

NeuroDiary is an open-source health diary focused on long-term monitoring of neurological diseases, especially Parkinson's disease.

Its goal is not only to store health records, but to transform them into useful information for patients and physicians.

## Problem

Many patients keep paper notes or use generic note-taking apps. Such data are difficult to analyze, share, and visualize over time.

NeuroDiary addresses this by providing a structured health diary.

## Core Principles

### Offline First
Core functionality works without an Internet connection.

### Privacy First
Users own their data. Cloud synchronization is optional.

### Open Source
The project is community-driven and extensible.

### Data Ownership
Data can always be exported. No vendor lock-in.

## Long-term Vision

NeuroDiary aims to become a platform for tracking multiple chronic diseases through modular extensions.

## What NeuroDiary is Not

- It is not a medical diagnosis tool.
- It does not replace physicians.
- It provides evidence, trends and reports—not medical decisions.
